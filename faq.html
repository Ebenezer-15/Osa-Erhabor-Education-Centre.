Q. When was Osa-Erhabor Education Centre established?

A. The school was founded in 2019.

Q. How can I register ?

A.Osa-Erhabor Education Centre basically has two policies runnning her registeration; the first is for the basic education which is from Kindergaten one (1) up till primary 5, you are to pay a one time committment fee of fifty thousand naira (50,000), then pay a maintenance fee of seven thousand (7,000) every term, while for the secondary school the registration fee is to be paid and the school fees.

Q. Meaning of the school name ?

A. Osa-Erhabor means "God my father favoured me", it is from the Edo Language.

Q. What's the reason for the policy the basic education has ?

A. Osa-Erhabor Education Centre has a vision of running a school where both the rich and the poor could afford and still have a great educational standard attained.
